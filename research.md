# Research

My research focuses on developing intelligent systems that integrate computer vision, machine learning, and human-computer interaction. I explore how these technologies can be combined to create adaptive, privacy-preserving, and sustainable solutions for real-world applications.

## Research Directions

### 1. Predictive World Models for Social Navigation

**Conference:** UKCI 2023  
**Status:** Published

This research focuses on developing predictive models that enable autonomous systems and robots to navigate safely in social environments. The work addresses the challenge of predicting human behavior and adapting navigation strategies accordingly.

**Key Contributions:**
- Development of predictive world models for social navigation
- Integration of human behavior prediction in navigation algorithms
- Evaluation in real-world social scenarios

**Publications:**
- Predictive World Models for Social Navigation (UKCI 2023)

---

### 2. Advanced Neural Network Architectures

**Focus:** Sequential Learning and Pattern Recognition

I work on developing novel neural network architectures, particularly focusing on gating mechanisms for improved sequential learning. My research includes the development of Cosine Gated LSTM models that demonstrate superior performance in complex sequential data processing.

**Key Contributions:**
- Development of Cosine Gated LSTM architecture
- Application to sequential learning tasks
- Performance analysis and optimization

**Publications:**
- Cosine Gated LSTM for Sequential Learning

---

### 3. Healthcare Technology Applications

**Focus:** Web-Based Healthcare Resource Discovery

I design and implement web-based systems for healthcare resource discovery and management. This work combines modern web technologies (React, Node.js) with user-centered design to create accessible and efficient healthcare information systems.

**Key Contributions:**
- Development of DiscoverHealth platform
- Integration of geographic information systems
- User-centered design and evaluation

**Projects:**
- DiscoverHealth: Healthcare Resource Discovery System
