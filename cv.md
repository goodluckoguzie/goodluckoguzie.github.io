## Education

### PhD in Computer Science
**Aston University, United Kingdom**  
**2020 – 2024**

**Thesis:** "Enhancing Robot Social Navigation with Reinforcement Learning and Advanced Predictive Models"

**Supervisors:** Dr. Luis J. Manso, Prof. Dr. Aniko Ekart

**Key Contributions:**
1. Predictive World Models (2StepAhead, MASPM) for multi-step ahead predictions
2. Cosine-Gated LSTM (CGLSTM) architecture for improved sequential learning
3. Adaptive Prediction Horizons that dynamically adjust based on environmental complexity

**Results:**
- Superior performance in LunarLander-v2 and SocNavGym environments
- Higher success rates compared to baseline methods
- More efficient navigation with adaptive prediction horizons
